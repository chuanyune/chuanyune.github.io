About Me

I am Chuanyu Li, an undergraduate student at Sichuan University. The other person in the profile photo is my girlfriend. I am also a visiting student at the RVSA Lab at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Rui Chen. My research interests focus on robotic manipulation and tactile sensing. I'm particularly passionate about developing robust manipulation skills for robots using vision-based tactile feedback.

ManiSkill-ViTac 2025: Challenge on Manipulation Skill Learning With Vision and Tactile Sensing

The ManiSkill-ViTac Challenge aims to push the boundaries of contact-rich manipulation skill learning with tactile and vision sensing. Building upon last year's challenge, the challenge 2025 extends the scope and includes three independent tracks, each focusing on different aspects of skill learning.

ManiSkill-ViTac: Vision-based-Tactile Manipulation Skill Learning Challenge 2024

The ManiSkill-ViTac Challenge aims to provide a standardized benchmarking platform for evaluating the performance of vision-based-tactile manipulation skill learning in real-world robot applications.
